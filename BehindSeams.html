Behind The Seams:

First Costume Backstory

The Green Curtain Dress

I decided to buy a princess costume pattern from Joann Fabrics and make adjustments based on the green curtain design. I also found these great curtain tassells at my local fabric store that were super inexpensive that really helped me nail my design. The most fun I had was creating the velvet bonnet with feathers. I would often reference costume photos to make sure I stayed as authentic as possible to the original.

Little by little day by day it all came together. It was a true labor of love but if there a will, there is a way and I found mine.
Hopefully, this may also inspire you to get creative with the costume of your dreams. :)
