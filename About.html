How Did the Halloween Queen Get Started?

Creating a MasterPiece

It's always so frustrating to trying to find the right costume. Sometimes we find you something in the store its often not as authentic or exquisite as the Original Movie Costume. I thought to myself, I could do costume more justice by creating it myself. Once I got that notion in my head, I could not let it go. Call me a little obsessive but I started to flow the designs and the materials needed. Then I would look online for inspiration and design to create my piece.
